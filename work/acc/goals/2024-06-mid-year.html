Behavior Strengths 

I excel in taking on new opportunities and tough challenges with a sense of urgency, high energy, and enthusiasm. I tackle every project quickly and efficiently, often taking on more holistic tasks that fall within the gaps between the responsibilities of team members. One of my greatest strengths is my ability to help prioritize and triage when competing requests arise. By applying clarification and focus, I ensure projects are completed with humanly achievable milestones, without the need to rush or overlook details.

Behavior Growth Opportunities 

This is the area where I see the greatest need for improvement. While I am strong in adaptability, communication, and collaboration when projects are in the solution stage, I need to improve in the earlier stages of projects and conversations.

I find that having a clear endpoint is incredibly helpful for me, but this can sometimes make other team members feel rushed, unheard, or that I am dismissing their points. I need to become more aware of team members who may have this conflict with my style of thinking and work on suppressing my tendencies. This will allow others to come up to speed or reach a point where they are receptive to solutions.
