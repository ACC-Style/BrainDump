UI Framework for Exclusive Member Benefit Access

Overview

The UI is designed to dynamically guide users through a personalized onboarding process tailored to their current state of access. By highlighting benefits, showcasing missing opportunities, and providing clear next steps, the page encourages users to advance their membership journey. This system supports four user states:

  1. Logged Out
  2. Logged In, Not a Member
  3. Logged In Member, Not Part of the Section
  4. Full Access

Document Outline by UI Sections and User States

UI Sections

  1. Primary CTA

    • Quick Headline: Unlock Access Now
    • Supportive Sentence: Discover the benefits waiting for you—resources, connections, and tools built for your success.
    • CTA Button: [Log In / Join Today]

  2. General Introduction

    • Global & Evergreen:
      • Welcome message emphasizing exclusive resources, tools, and opportunities.
      • Encourages users to unlock the next level of their career.

  3. Footer Echoed CTA

    • Global & Evergreen:
      • Headline: Log In Now!
      • Supportive Description: Don’t miss out—log in today to unlock exclusive resources.
      • CTA Button: [Log In]

  4. Lock Statement

    • Global & Evergreen:
      • Headline: Only Available to Members of ACC
      • Description: Highlights the exclusivity of Member Sections and benefits of ACC membership.
      • Encourages logging in to explore resources and perks.
      • CTA Button: [Learn More]

  5. Convincing Statement to Pass the Lock

    • Not Evergreen: Varies depending on user state & Section.
      • For Logged Out: Encourages logging in to unlock opportunities.
      • For Logged In, Not a Member: Explains the benefits of ACC membership.
      • For Logged In Member, Not Part of the Section: Describes the value of joining the specific section.

  6. In-depth Section

    • Not Evergreen: Detailed content for specific sections (e.g., Women in Cardiology).
    • Includes tailored benefits, mission statements, and descriptions to persuade users.
    • Ends with a specific CTA for the section.

User States

  1. Logged Out

    • Primary CTA: Encourages logging in.
    • Lock Statement: Focuses on logging in to explore.
    • Convincing Statement: Describes benefits of logging in.

  2. Logged In, Not a Member

    • Primary CTA: Highlights joining ACC to access exclusive benefits.
    • Lock Statement: Emphasizes becoming a member.
    • Convincing Statement: Details the advantages of membership (tools, network, and career growth).

  3. Logged In Member, Not Part of the Section

    • Primary CTA: Encourages joining the specific section.
    • Lock Statement: Highlights section exclusivity and alignment with user goals.
    • Convincing Statement: Outlines tailored benefits (e.g., networking, resources, leadership pathways).

  4. Full Access

    • No additional content required beyond acknowledgment of full access.
