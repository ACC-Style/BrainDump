Introduction to CCKE

The Certified Cardiovascular Knowledge Examination (CCKE) is designed for healthcare professionals who want to demonstrate their expertise in cardiovascular medicine. It is an opportunity for NPs, PAs, and other clinicians to validate their knowledge, enhance their careers, and contribute to the standard of excellence in cardiovascular care.

To learn more about the application process, eligibility requirements, and how to apply, continue exploring our resources.

Benefits of Taking the Exam

  • Validate your clinical knowledge
  • Boost job prospects in cardiology
  • Uphold excellence in patient care
  • Earn credits towards FACC/AACC
  • Advance your career and enhance your reputation
  • Be recognized as an expert in CV care
